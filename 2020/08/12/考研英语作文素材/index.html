考研英语作文素材
发表于2020-08-12|更新于2020-08-12|考研英语
|字数总计:1.3k|阅读时长:6分钟|阅读量:

在背完上篇模板后，可以背一下素材，考研英语一大作文常考话题主要包括文化类、精神品质（人生哲理）、社会现象（比如两代关系、偶像崇拜等）、科技类、环境类，这样划分算是比较细的了，第一段直接背诵模板即可，第二、三段主要是围绕文章主题展开，不建议用简单句，这里建议每个话题背诵一个素材，考到的花基本上换个词就可以了，从2000到目前，考研英语一大作文都是考的以下话题，所以一共几个素材，都背诵一下，基本上就可以看到话题不用思考直接写，考研英语作文比较开放，没有统一的中心思想，所以将以下素材背的熟练，基本上考什么都可以嵌套，请看以下素材（素材整理于王江涛老师的《考研英语高分写作》）:

精神品质（人生哲理）

此部分和上篇模板重复，把它放在这里的第一个，足以显示它的重要性，不啰嗦了，一定要背熟！！！内容较少，可以自己加点谚语什么的

二段：From the picture we can conclude tat the painter wants to convey such a message： in the face of seemingly insurmountable challenges or predicaments，possessing （主题词）is of utmost significance. Regardless of how much success one has had in schooling or at work, there are bound to be times when he or she will encounter problems. It is the critical times that one’s （主题词），toward difficulty and toward life in generally，plays a crucial role in determining whether he or she can turn failure into victory.

三段：In my view that we can use the picture to aid youngers in learning to （主题相关），（主题词）is one of the most important elements in deciding our personal growth. Whatever difficulties or situations we are confronted with，we should remind ourselves that those who have the spirit of （主题词）are nearer to success.

文化类

当下热点，全背下来的话考到此话题全文可用。

二段：From the picture we can conclude tat the painter wants to convey such a message： The fact that people from different countries are attracted to the mysterious Chinese culture indicates that to some extent a culture can be accepted, respected, appreciated and shared internationally. In other words, a nation’s unique culture can become international through worldwide economic and cultural exchange. Since the trend of globalization has become irresistible, increasing cultural exchange can effectively improve mutual understanding and friendship between countries.

三段：In my view that we can use the picture to aid youngers in learning that there are good reasons to advocate for internationalized culture, for those ideas from other cultures, though controversial or even absurd at first sight, can in the long run provide a different perspective for us to observe the world. Nevertheless, when we are confronted with a different culture, we should be sensible enough to absorb its virtues and to resist its dark side. Only in this way can we make our world colorful.

科技类

比较热，近几年也考过，关于网络类、技术类都可以用这个这个素材一Internet为例，如果考偶像崇拜，可以参考三段，理智追星。

二段：The picture subtly reflect the social phenomenon that the internet is booming everywhere（自己换） On the one hand, there is no denying that the Internet is currently one of the most efficient media used for interpersonal communication. As a college student, I get on line every day to discuss news with other people on internet, to study English by registering for web courses, and to chat freely with my friends through QQ Messenger. But on the other hand, a good many people admit that they are too addicted to the Internet to maintain face-to-face contact with their friends and colleagues. Once having indulged in the fictitious world, people feel reluctant to approach others and to concentrate on real life. That’s why some people have lost the skill of direct contact and become alienated from others

三段：In my view that we can use the picture to aid youngers in learning that it is necessary for us to use the Internet in a reasonable way and restrain from overindulgence. After all, the Internet was invented to connect you and me, and to bring conveniences to our life rather than setting barriers to keep people beyond reach.

两代关系

此话题就写怎么和长辈相处，孝顺父母、尊敬长辈就可以了

二段：The picture subtly reflect the social phenomenon that the relationship between elder and their sons or daughters(看图片是什么)，As a nation renowned worldwide for its many virtues, China and the Chinese people have been practicing filial piety throughout history. such as the story of Tiying in the Western Han Dynasty who risked her life in order to save her wronged father from corporal punishment. Those who try every means to avoid their duties of looking after their elders should bear in mind that they are much indebted to their parents for their rearing. Without the loving care and selfless devotion of their parents, how could they have grown up healthily and become successful? How is it possible for them to maltreat their parents without the pricks of conscience?

三段：In my view that we can use the picture to aid youngers in learning to how get along with their parents，Every one of us should live up to the virtues passed down to us by our ancestors. Only in this way can we be worthy of the name of a Chinese.

环境类

本素材是以经旅游业展开，自己可重点背几个句子

二段：From the picture, we can conclude that the painter wants to convey such a message: a good many scenic spots are flooded with visitors, who spoil the beauty of nature by creating and leaving behind God-knows-how-much trash. On the one hand, tourism, as a multibillion-dollar business, is booming everywhere. Some people, under the belief that tourism serves as an engine of economic growth, seem to ignore its negative effects on the environment. Nevertheless, these are not concerns that we can shrug off lightly. On the other hand,tourism has exerted great pressure on the environment w上e are living in: our water is polluted, our ecological system is disturbed, and our natural resources have been excessively used.

三段：Undoubtedly, tourism cannot be banned in any country, as it does help to shore up the economy in places which have few sources of income. However, the main point is that the unchecked growth of the travel business may render the development of an economy unsustainable. It is high time that we enhanced people’s awareness to rectify this by taking environmental protection into consideration.
